About Brabus

Brabus is a leading German high-performance automotive engineering and design company. The company was founded in 1970 by Bodo Buschmann and Klaus Brackmanand. The current CEO is Constantin Buschmann. Brabus is known for modifying and customizing cars and trucks of various makes and models. BRABUS offers a wide range of products and services, including engine tuning, suspension systems, exhaust systems, and interior design. The company has a global presence and has dealerships in many countries around the world.
